Who are we

We're the Wellington Queer Students Association; a group of lgbtqia+ high school students who are driven by a desire to improve the school experience for queer students. We believe that we need a place run by students that is for students. No one knows the current experience as a queer student better than current queer students themselves. We are all very passionate to find small ways in our community to greater the quality of life in our community.
