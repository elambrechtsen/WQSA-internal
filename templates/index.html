        Who are we

        We're the Wellington Queer Students Association; a group of lgbtqia+ high school students who are driven by a desire to improve the school experience for queer students. We believe that we need a place run by students that is for students. No one knows the current experience as a queer student better than current queer students themselves. We are all very passionate to find small ways in our community to greater the quality of youth life in our community. “We're here. We're queer.”

          Photos taken at Out in the city

          Wellington Pride organises a range of pride events for the queer community in Wellington. These events are run by the Wellington city council and Out in the city happens every year and is a wonderful time to feel comforable in your queer identity.
